Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Typing Master collects, uses, and protects your data.

What We Collect

  • Typing Game Data: High scores and settings stored in your browser’s local storage.
  • Contact Form Info: Name and email collected to respond to your inquiries.
  • Site Usage: Anonymous data on page visits to improve our site.

How We Use It

  • Display saved scores and preferences.
  • Respond to contact form submissions.
  • Analyze site usage to enhance user experience.

Sharing Info

We don’t sell or share personal data. Anonymous data may be used by third-party analytics under strict privacy rules.

Cookies & Storage

We use localStorage to save settings and scores, not cookies. We don’t track or advertise based on your activity.

Your Choices

  • Clear scores/settings in the game anytime.
  • Email us to delete contact form data.

Questions?

Contact us via our contact page. We’re here to help.
