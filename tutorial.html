Tutorial

Get started with Typing Master! This tutorial will guide you through using our fun and engaging typing game to improve your typing skills.

How to Play

  • Start the Game: On the homepage, click the "Start Typing" button to begin a 25-second typing test.
  • Type the Text: A random text will appear. Type it as accurately and quickly as possible in the input box provided.
  • Track Your Progress: Your words per minute (WPM), accuracy, and score will be calculated in real-time.
  • View Results: After the timer ends, a results screen will show your WPM, accuracy, and high score.

Tips for Success

  • Practice Regularly: Short, daily sessions can significantly boost your typing speed and accuracy.
  • Adjust Settings: Use the settings menu to customize text difficulty or enable/disable features like sound effects.
  • Stay Relaxed: Keep your hands relaxed to type more comfortably and avoid fatigue.

Saving Your Progress

Your high scores and settings are saved in your browser’s local storage, so you can pick up where you left off. Clear them anytime via the settings menu if needed.
